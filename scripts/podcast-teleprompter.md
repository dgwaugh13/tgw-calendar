• So we look in 3 places, in order ↓

CHART 1 — REVENUE (the payoff lands first):
• It's showing up, and it's SPEEDING UP.
  say: "Azure up 40. Google Cloud up 63.
        AWS up 28 — fastest in 15 quarters.
        Nvidia data center up 92."
• Point: demand is real, they can't build fast enough.

CHART 2 — MARGINS (is it in profit?):
• Profits still GREW ~30% at all four.
• Margins holding — even rising.
• Catch: depreciation hasn't fully hit yet. Watch this.

CHART 3 — FREE CASH FLOW (the squeeze):
• THIS is the honest one.
  say: "Microsoft's free cash flow — down 22%.
        Alphabet — cut roughly in half.
        Amazon — down 95%."
• Earnings look great while CASH gets drained. That's capex.

CHART 4 — NVIDIA (the other side):
• Their spending IS Nvidia's revenue.
  say: "Data center up 92, margins around 75%."
• Caveat: it's a little circular — same names buying each other.
