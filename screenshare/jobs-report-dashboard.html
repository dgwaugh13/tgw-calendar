What the print means for the Fed

Tie it straight back to Monday's dot plot — 9 saw a hike, 1 a cut.

▲ HOT jobs (strong + wages up)

The hike camp gets its ammo. "Good news is bad news" — rate-cut hopes fade, rate-sensitive stocks can pull back. Higher-for-longer looks more likely.

▼ COOL jobs (soft + wages tame)

The cut camp wins the argument. Markets relax on hopes the Fed eases — but a too-cold print raises slowdown worries. Read the whole picture, not one line.
