The frame for the audience

Rotation ≠ exit. Money left one lane and showed up in another the same day. You don't have to predict the next lane — a diversified base + automatic buys owns the leaders before they lead. Watch: relative strength, the new 52-week-high list, and volume. Chart literacy, not predictions.
