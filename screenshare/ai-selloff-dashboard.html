Spending vs payoff — still the real story

Why the tape whipsaws both ways

The spend

~$452B of 2026 capex across Microsoft, Alphabet, Amazon and Meta — data centers, chips, power.

The payoff question

Last week the market wanted proof; today it gave the benefit of the doubt. Unlike dot-com, these firms are profitable — so sentiment, not solvency, is driving the swings. Watch free cash flow, not the daily candle.
