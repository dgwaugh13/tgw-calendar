What the print means for the Fed

Tie it straight back to the dot plot — 9-to-1 toward a hike.

▲ HOT core PCE

Sticky underlying inflation. The hike camp gets its ammo; "good news is bad news" — rate-cut hopes fade, rate-sensitive stocks can wobble. Higher-for-longer looks more likely.

▼ COOL core PCE

The energy spike was the story, not the trend. Cut hope returns and markets relax — but read the whole picture, not one line.

What to actually watch (not the headline)
