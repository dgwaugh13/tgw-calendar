The gap is energy. Headline includes food + energy; core strips them out. The Fed — and you — should read core.

What's dragging the headline up

Energy is the outlier, on the Iran-conflict oil shock.

A single input can hijack a headline. Energy was +17.9% in April → +23.5% in May. A peace deal is now easing oil — watch for the reversal.
