# TGW Evergreen Reel Bank

A standing library of evergreen IG/TikTok reels the weekly engine pulls from to fill the **non-news slots** (the days that aren't a concrete news-peg or the chart evergreen). News-pegged reactions, the weekly chart-read evergreen, and the collab/reaction beat still lead the week — this bank fills the gaps and gives us recordable backups so we're never scrambling.

> Source batch: `tgw-35-reel-batch.md` (record-day shot list). Educational content only — not financial advice; no tickers, no price targets, no "this will go up."

## How the weekly engine uses this bank (read every run)
- **Pull 1–2 per week** into the open evergreen slots, and rotate so nothing repeats inside ~8 weeks (track in "Rotation log" at the bottom — mark the IDs used each week).
- **Lead with TIER 1 on Instagram.** Our data says story/personal + debate/comment-trigger reels are the only evergreen talking-head content that lives near our IG winners; generic explainers are our weakest IG tier.
- **Generic explainers → TikTok Search filler**, not IG headliners.
- **Match the CTA rhythm:** soft on weekdays, comment-trigger on the high-engagement ones (10, 14, 18, 22, 24, 32, 34), hard DM (35) weekend only.
- **One live number:** #24 needs the current average credit-card APR — see the APR note under that reel; re-verify before posting.
